quick answers

you probably want to know.

does this cost anything?+
nope. you need a Claude account (free tier works) and that's it. no subscription, no hidden fees, nothing.
where does my data go?+
everything stays on your device. the tracker uses localStorage. nothing is uploaded anywhere. it's just a file on your phone.
what if I don't like what it builds?+
start a new Claude chat and paste the prompt again. give different answers, pick a different aesthetic. you can do this as many times as you want.
does it work on iPhone?+
yes. open the HTML file in Safari. works perfectly. you can even save it to your home screen so it feels like an app.
can I pick any theme I want?+
yes. standard themes, character themes like Batman or Powerpuff Girls, or describe literally anything. Tokyo at night. Y2K. your favourite anime. Claude will build the whole thing around it.
what if I want my own stickers in it?+
go to Pinterest or Google Images, find images you love, then go to remove.bg to remove the background. send the PNG files to Claude during the chat and it'll place them in meaningful spots throughout your planner.
